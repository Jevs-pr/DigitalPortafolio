Culture Party

  • Category: Game
  • Role: Programmer , Design UI and Music
  • Engine: Unreal Engine 4
  • Language: Ue4 Blueprint & Ue4 c++
  • Class: Capstone
  • Project date: May, 2021 - Nov , 2021

An online game party in the style of a board game. Presenting a idea of various cultures of the world in a unique game created by a small group. Whether players come together and complete different types of mini games or events that get you some coins to buy trophies and win the game.
