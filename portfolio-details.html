Culture Party

  • Category: Game
  • Engine: Unreal Engine 4
  • Language: Ue4 Blueprint & Ue4 c++
  • Class: Capstone
  • Project date: May, 2021

An online game party in the style of a board game. Presenting a idea of various cultures of the world in a unique game created by a small group. Whether players come together and complete different types of mini games or events that get you some coins to buy trophies and win the game. This project was made for a class of ‘Capstone 1’ in the Atlantic University College, however it has not been completed as it requires to finish another class of ‘Capstone 2’. Most of the project was done with Unreal Engine Blueprint.
