The story tells that in the distance of some islands in the sky, a former warrior called Notender, from the past wars and adventures that lay along across the sky was in his peaceful house in the clouds when from the nothing notices that in the sky he is plagued with creatures that before lived there, and now Notender fears losing his home and decides to return to Prepare for the battle that awaits you to regain the peace of the kingdom from him. In the battle was not heard from him anymore, only that he fought for peace in the clouds while in an ambush no more was heard from him, that's where he barely receives that news Dylan his son decides to prepare and go to battle in the search for his father in the islands of the sky. This game was made for class from Creation of videogames prototypes at Atlantic University College. Most of this game was made in c ++
